Journey Through the Underworld: A Greco-Roman Adventure

Based on Homer's Odyssey and Virgil's Aeneid

Dido

Queen of Carthage, forsaken by Aeneas, seeking closure or vengeance.

Anchises

Aeneas's father, now a soul in Elysium with knowledge of Rome's future.

The Sibyl

Prophetic priestess of Apollo, guides heroes through the dark.

Tiresias

Blind prophet, consulted by Odysseus to learn his fate.

Achilles

Once the greatest Greek warrior, now dwelling in the fields of Asphodel.

Anticlea

Odysseus's mother, bearer of familial wisdom and personal sorrow.
